🧴 Tattoo Aftercare Guide: How to Heal Your Tattoo Properly

Getting a tattoo is exciting, but proper aftercare is essential to ensure it heals well and looks great for years to come. Here's a step-by-step guide to caring for your new ink.

✨ 1. Right After the Tattoo Session

  • Leave the bandage on for 2 to 24 hours, as advised by your artist.
  • Avoid touching or scratching the area with unwashed hands.

💦 2. First Wash (After Removing the Bandage)

When: 2 to 24 hours after getting the tattoo.

  1. Wash your hands thoroughly.
  2. Gently wash the tattoo using lukewarm water and fragrance-free, antibacterial soap.
  3. Pat dry with a clean paper towel or let it air dry.
  4. Let your tattoo breathe for a bit before applying ointment.

🧴 3. Applying Aftercare Ointment

Frequency: 2-3 times daily for the first 3-5 days.

  • Use a thin layer of a recommended tattoo aftercare product.
  • Do not over-apply — it can suffocate the tattoo and delay healing.

🌿 4. The Peeling Phase (Days 4-14)

  • Your tattoo will scab or peel — don't pick or scratch.
  • Keep the area moisturized with a light, fragrance-free lotion.
  • Continue gentle washing once or twice a day.

☀️ 5. General Healing Tips (First 2-4 Weeks)

✅ Do:

  • Wear loose, breathable clothing.
  • Stay hydrated and eat well.

❌ Don't:

  • No swimming in pools, hot tubs, lakes, or oceans.
  • No direct sunlight or tanning on the tattoo.
  • Avoid heavy workouts for the first few days.

⏳ 6. Long-Term Care (After It Heals)

  • Moisturize regularly to keep skin healthy and vibrant.
  • Use sunscreen (SPF 30+) whenever the tattoo is exposed to the sun.

🩺 When to Contact a Professional

  • Severe redness, swelling, or warmth around the tattoo
  • Foul odor or pus
  • Red streaks or fever/chills
